FAQ

Common Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

+

Yes. You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time — the change takes effect from the next billing cycle. Downgrades are also possible with 30 days' written notice. Any ongoing campaigns or deliverables will be completed before the downgrade takes effect.

What currency are the prices in?

+

All prices are in Armenian Dram (AMD) and are billed monthly. Invoices are issued in AMD. If you require billing in another currency, please contact us to discuss arrangements.

How does the 5% e-commerce revenue share work on Tier 2?

+

The 5% applies to gross e-commerce revenue generated through the website or digital assets we build and manage for you. Revenue is tracked via agreed analytics tools and invoiced within the first 5 business days of each following month, in addition to the monthly retainer.

What is the Zontik.com listing and how does it help?

+

Zontik.com is a fair discovery platform where local businesses can be found without competing against giant ad budgets. Your business is listed with a profile, tags, and a direct link to your website. There are no algorithms or paid placements — every business is listed fairly, so your listing has equal visibility.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment period?

+

Our monthly plans are billed month-to-month with no long-term contract. You can cancel with 30 days' written notice. Project-based work (e.g. one-off website builds) follows the timeline in the agreed scope of work.

Do you offer custom packages for larger businesses?

+

Absolutely. If none of the three tiers fit your specific needs, contact us and we'll put together a custom proposal. We work with businesses of all sizes and can tailor scope, deliverables, and pricing accordingly.
